Our Grand Mission

A collaborative partnership rooted in shared growth, learning, and transparent communication.

ABLE Partners’ Mission

ABLE Partners was created when the principals recognized they possessed mutually supportive skills, resources, and aspirations. The partnership already had a solid, compatible human foundation on which to build.

Enjoying shared objectives and compatible values of cooperation, open and transparent dealings, the partners decided to pool complementary resources to build a shared vision of business without borders—Side By Side.

Our mission is to cultivate a community where cooperation and mutual respect drive the co-creation of sustainable, financially viable digital assets. We believe in building a broader digital community of like minds and values; builders and users alike embracing the principles of openness and collective growth.

We aim to create lasting value that benefits all stakeholders in this broader community that we want to serve. Leveraging AI tools, we are designing platforms that will serve both literate and illiterate members of that community.

Our choice of the .SBS domain—symbolizing "Side by Side"—reflects our dedication to standing in solidarity with those who share our vision for a more inclusive and ethical digital economy. Together, we strive to make exploitation of gig workers uneconomic, promote shared ownership, and achieve a sustainable future through collaborative innovation using many affordable tools emerging daily within the AI ecosphere.

In summary, we are creating web-based resources that will empower especially economically disadvantaged communities.

Why an .SBS Domain for our Focal Communications Platform?

Our mission embodies our core values at ABLE Partners.

We were surprised—then elated—to find that there exists a lesser-known TLD (Top Level Domain) that was consistent with and effortlessly reflects our philosophy.

We discovered the motivation underlying the creation of the .SBS TLD Registry! It is well articulated here.

It gave the perfect strategic orientation to our Strategic Plan. It promotes and signifies inclusivity, accentuates sustainability, and inspires doing more and better in united action. We were immediately convinced of its appropriateness.

It fittingly anchors ABLE Partners’ web presence:

  • It actually describes—even advertises—our mission, and
  • Innately appeals to all of our target communities, internationally and locally.

The .SBS domain and its ICANN Registrar’s community-driven philosophy—Side By Side—emphasizes our commitment to ethical collaboration, sustainable growth, and communal solutions.

For these salient reasons, we consciously chose to operate our Ethiopian-Canadian venture with a Top Level Domain, AblePartners.SBS, that projects our values.
